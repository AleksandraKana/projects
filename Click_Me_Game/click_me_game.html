Click Me Game

Instructions

  • Click anywhere to start the game.
  • A colored box will appear at a random position inside the game area.
  • Click on the box as fast as you can!
  • You only have 1 second to click before the game ends.
  • Each successful click increases your score, and a new box appears.
  • If you miss clicking within 1 second, the game is over!
  • Click the Restart Game button to play again.
